Teaching

Benjamin teaches piano both privately and also for the following Music Services/Schools:

  • Arts Academy
  • The Music House for Children
  • The Eltham Foundation School
  • Sound Generation
Benjamin charges £40.00 per hour and teaches people of all ages and abilities. If you are interested in having private tuition with Benjamin, please contact him using the details provided in the 'Contact' section.

Benjamin's CV is available for download here.

Benjamin is a member of the Incorporated Society of Musicians, the UK's professional body for musicians.
